⚖️ Divorce Costs — Ireland 2026

How Much Does a
Divorce Solicitor Cost in Ireland?

The cost of a divorce solicitor in Ireland depends more on the level of dispute than anything else. An agreed, uncontested divorce can be relatively straightforward. A contested divorce involving property, pensions, and custody can become very expensive. Knowing the difference changes how you plan.

Outcome

Because both parties had already agreed all terms through mediation, the solicitor work was limited to drafting the consent order and making the court application. Each party paid approximately €1,400 in legal fees. The divorce was granted without a contested hearing.
