Personal Injury Solicitor
Costs in Ireland. No Win No Fee.

Personal injury solicitors in Ireland almost always work on a no win no fee basis — meaning you pay nothing upfront and nothing if you lose. But understanding what percentage they take, and what costs you might still be responsible for, is important before you sign anything.

How Aoife's situation resolved

Outcome

Aoife's solicitor submitted a PIAB application with medical reports and evidence of loss of earnings. PIAB assessed the claim and awarded €28,000. The solicitor advised Aoife to accept. After the solicitor's fee of 25%, Aoife received approximately €21,000. Her total out-of-pocket cost before the settlement was zero.
