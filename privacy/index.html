Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

eSolicitors.ie Ltd operates the website at esolicitors.ie. We are a solicitor referral and matching service registered in Ireland. Our registered address is 3 The Turnpike, Ballymun, Dublin 11, Ireland.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018, eSolicitors.ie Ltd is the data controller of personal data collected through this website.

What data we collect

When you use our solicitor matching service, we collect the following personal data:

  • Your first name
  • Your phone number
  • A description of your legal situation (as provided by you in your own words)
  • The county or location you provide
  • Technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited (collected automatically)

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as health data, financial data, or criminal record data) unless you choose to include it in your description of your legal situation.

How we use your data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To match you with a qualified solicitor in your county who handles your type of case
  • To pass your contact details to the matched solicitor so they can contact you directly
  • To improve our matching service and website
  • To comply with our legal obligations

Our legal basis for processing your data is your consent, given when you submit your details through our matching service. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us at info@esolicitors.ie.

Who we share your data with

We share your personal data only with the solicitor or legal practice we match you with. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with marketing companies or data brokers.

We use Google Sheets to store lead data securely. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf and processes data in accordance with Google's data processing terms.

Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel processes data in accordance with GDPR as part of their data processing agreement.

How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for a maximum of 12 months from the date it was submitted, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After this period, your data is deleted from our systems.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you can object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@esolicitors.ie. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission of Ireland at dataprotection.ie.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for the operation of the site. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics service that tracks individual users.
