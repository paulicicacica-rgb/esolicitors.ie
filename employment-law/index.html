Critical Deadlines

Employment law has strict time limits

Miss these windows and your claim cannot proceed, regardless of how strong it is. Act as soon as something goes wrong.

Unfair Dismissal
6 months
From date of dismissal. Extendable to 12 months in exceptional cases only.
Discrimination
6 months
From the most recent act of discrimination. Extendable to 12 months in exceptional cases.
Redundancy Pay
52 weeks
From date of dismissal to bring a claim for statutory redundancy payment.
Unpaid Wages
6 months
From the most recent underpayment. Arrears can be claimed going back further.
Whistleblower Penalisation
6 months
From the date of penalisation. No minimum service period required.
Pregnancy Dismissal
6 months
Automatically unfair. No minimum service period. Act immediately.
