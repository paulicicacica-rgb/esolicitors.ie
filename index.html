Don't Wait — Legal Deadlines in Ireland Are Strict

Many legal claims in Ireland have strict time limits. Personal injury claims must be filed within 2 years of the incident. Employment claims with the WRC must be made within 6 months. Miss these windows and your case is gone — permanently. If something happened to you, get advice now, not later.
