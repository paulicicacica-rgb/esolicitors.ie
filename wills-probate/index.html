Wills and probate
in Ireland.
Protect what matters.

A will is one of the most important legal documents you will ever make. Without one, the law decides where your estate goes — and it may not go where you want. For those dealing with a death, the probate process involves specific legal steps, Revenue obligations, and real deadlines.

  • Make a valid will that reflects your wishes
  • Understand the probate process after a death
  • Contest a will on legal grounds
  • Calculate your inheritance tax position
  • Protect a cohabiting partner or non-family member
