eSolicitors.ie is an Irish solicitor matching service. We help people across all 26 counties find qualified legal help — without the confusion, the cold calls, or the cost.

Who We Are

A free matching service for Irish people who need legal help

eSolicitors.ie was built around a simple idea: finding a good solicitor in Ireland should not be complicated, expensive, or intimidating. Most people facing a legal problem do not know where to start. They search online, find a list of firms, and have no way of knowing who is right for their situation.

We fix that. You describe your situation — in plain English, no legal terms needed — and we match you with a qualified Irish solicitor in your county who specialises in exactly your type of case. The first consultation is always free. There is no obligation to proceed.

We cover all 26 counties and eight practice areas including personal injury, employment law, property law, family law, criminal law, immigration, wills and probate, and debt.

How It Works

Three steps. No forms. No awkward cold calls.

Step 1 — Tell us what happened. Describe your situation to Sarah, our intake assistant. Plain language is fine. You do not need to know any legal terms.

Step 2 — We match you. Sarah identifies the right solicitor in your county who handles exactly your type of case and collects your contact details.

Step 3 — Your solicitor calls you back. A qualified Irish solicitor contacts you directly. The first consultation is free. There is no obligation to proceed or to instruct them.

How We Make Money

Solicitors pay us. You never do.

eSolicitors.ie is free to use for the public. We are funded by the solicitors and legal practices we refer cases to. When a referral leads to a case being instructed, the solicitor pays us a referral fee. You are never charged anything, at any point.

This model means our interests are aligned with yours — we only get paid when we make a successful match. We have no reason to match you with the wrong solicitor.

Legal Information

We are a referral service, not a law firm

eSolicitors.ie is a referral and information service. We are not a law firm and we do not provide legal advice. Nothing on this website constitutes legal advice and no solicitor-client relationship is created by using this service.

All solicitors we refer to are independently qualified and regulated by the Law Society of Ireland. We do not guarantee any particular outcome for your case.
